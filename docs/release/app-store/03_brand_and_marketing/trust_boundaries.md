# Trust Boundaries

Safe wording:

- Designed for Australian teachers.
- Built for Year 5 and Year 6 report comments.
- Uses familiar Australian report-comment language.
- The app helps prepare draft wording. The teacher checks every comment.
- The app does not upload student names or report notes to an online account.
- You choose when to save, export or share a file.
- Teachers should follow their school's reporting and privacy requirements.
- Local app data may be included in device or iCloud backups depending on the user's device settings, unless the app explicitly excludes it from backup.

Do not claim curriculum alignment, government approval, department
approval, school approval, universal policy compliance, perfect privacy,
or that teacher judgement is replaced.
