# FAQ

## Who is the app for?

Australian primary teachers writing Year 5 and Year 6 report comments.

## Does it need a sign-in?

No. There is no online account in the current app.

## Does it work without Wi-Fi?

Yes for the core report-comment path.

## Does it replace teacher judgement?

No. The app prepares draft wording. The teacher checks every comment.

## Can I use it for every school?

Follow your school's reporting and privacy requirements.
