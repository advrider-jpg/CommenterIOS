Report Comment Writer is for teachers, not students.

Teachers may enter student names, class names, achievement levels, teacher
notes, evidence, draft comments, checked comments, and exported report files.

No sign-in is required. The app does not create an online account. The app
does not upload student names or report notes to an online account.

The current repo does not configure analytics, tracking, ads, cloud sync,
subscriptions, or a remote server upload path. Re-check this before
submission.

The teacher chooses when to import, export, save, back up, restore, or share
files. User-created exports and backups go wherever the teacher chooses to
save or share them.

Local app data may be included in device or iCloud backups depending on the
user's device settings, unless the app explicitly excludes it from backup.

Teachers should follow their school's reporting and privacy requirements.
